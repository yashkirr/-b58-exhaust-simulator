How to interpret it

Deep dips in the graph indicate frequencies where the two paths partially cancel. Peaks indicate reinforcement. Move the extra branch length and watch the pattern shift. A design that looks dramatic around normal cruising frequencies may also create drone, so test several lengths rather than optimising for a single RPM.
